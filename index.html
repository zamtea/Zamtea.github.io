MIZO ṬAWNG ZIAH DANA GRAMMAR DAN AWM TE

Mizo ṭawng ziah dan tur chin fel nân hian grammar hi teh fung pawimawh ber a ni a. Ṭawng dangte ang bawkin Mizo Ṭawng hi a ziah dan turah grammar aṭangin kàl phung fel tak a awm a, chumi hmang chuan ‘Mizo Ṭawng Ziah Dan Dik’ hi rawn chhawp chhuah a ni a; chûng grammar aṭanga Mizo ṭawng ziah dan tur kàl phung dik, Mizo Grammar Society-in innghah nâna a hmante chu a pawimawh zual han târ lâng hmasa ila.

1. Noun leh noun lam kawp

Ziah zawm loh tur chite: Noun thu mal ve kan lam kawpnaah, noun thu mal hmasa zâwk chu a kawppui noun thu mal pakhat zâwk sawi fiahtu a nih chuan, a kawppui noun thu mal sawi fiahtu zâwk chu ‘nounal adjective’ (noun adjunct) tih a ni a. Chutianga noun thu mal ve inkawpah, pakhat zâwk nounal adjective a nih chuan, chûng noun thu mal inkawp chu noun leh noun-adjective ṭang dun noun phrase an nih avangin ziah zawm loh tur a ni.

Entirna: ar tui, âwm phaw, chaw thlèng, êk in, lal lukhum, hmai kâwr...etc.

Ziah zawm tur chite: Noun thu mal ve inkawpah, noun thu mal pakhat zâwk chu a kawppui noun thu mal sawi fiahtu (nounal adjective) a nih loh chuan, chûng noun thu mal inkawpte chu ziah zawma thu mal an nih a ngâi ṭhin a.

Entirna: kekawr, belthlèng, kutke, tarmit, nufa/pafa, tisa...etc.

2. Noun leh adjective lam kawp

Ziah zawm loh tur chite: Grammar danah noun leh adjective ṭang dun chu ‘noun phrase’ a nih ṭhin avangin ziah zawm loh tur a ni.

Entirna: chanchin thar, ruh rô, kawr lum, ro hlû, thu buai, ṭâng nêm...etc.

Ziah zawm tur chite: Noun leh adjective inkawp; amaherawhchu, kan sentence-a a inkawpa verb atâna kan hman ṭhin; nimahsela, verb (nounal verb) leh adverb (adjectival adverb) emaw, verb phrase emaw, phrasal verb emaw ni thei lo, thu mal atâna hman ngâi a awm a. Chûng chu ziah zawma thu mal a nihin a dik thung.

Entirna: hmelchhia, hmelṭha, thinchhia, rimtui/rimchhia, mangchhia, mangṭha...etc.

3. Noun leh Participle lam kawp

Ziah zawm loh tur chite: Noun thu mal leh verb thu mal kan lam kawpnaah, verb thu mal chu a kawppui noun awm dan leh nih dan sawi fiahtu a nih chuan ‘participle’ a ni ṭhin a, chutianga noun leh participle ṭang dun chu ‘noun phrase’ a nih avangin ziah zawm lohin a dik ṭhin.

Entirna: âr khuang, kâwl phe, lir nghing, mar phû, tlâng kàng, ṭêk tla...etc.

Participle hi noun thu mal hmakhalha lam hmasak ṭhin kan nei bawk a, chûng pawh chu ‘noun phrase’ an nih avangin ziah zawm loh a dik leh bawk.

Entirna: awm hmun, mut kawr, rin thu, thlen in, vawn fung, zin kawng...etc.

Ziah zawm tur chite: Noun thu mal leh verb thu mal inkawp; amaherawhchu, noun leh participle ni ve tawh lo a awm a, chûng chu thu mal an nih a ngâi thung a.

Entirna: bengbeh, nghawngawrh, kutziak, lukham, tukchhuak, zungbun...etc.

4. Noun leh postposition

Ziah zawm loh tur chite: Noun thu mal emaw, gerund emaw hi postposition nen emaw, postpositional noun nen emawa kan lam kawp chuan ‘noun phrase’ a ni ṭhin a, ziah zawm lohin a dik ṭhin.

Entirna: Pathian zâr, kawng chhak, in hnuai, ram tân, nun phung, Zuala âi...etc.

Ziah zawm tur chite: Postposition leh thu mal dang lam kawp; amaherawhchu, noun phrase ni ve si lo, awmze dang nei thu mal atâna ziah zawm ngâi a awm a.

Entirna: ramhnuai, kawrchung, kawrhnuai, rapchung/tapchhak, hmunhma, kawchhûng...etc.

5. Noun leh gender adjective

Ziah zawm loh tur chite: Noun thu mal leh gender adjective kan lam kawp ṭhin hi ‘noun phrase’ a ni ṭhin a, ziah zawm lohin a dik.

Entirna: bâwng pâ, bâwng nù, âr lâ, sakhi tûai, nghal chang, se chal...etc.

Ziah zawm tur chite: Gender adjective atâna kan hman ṭhin; amaherawhchu, a kawppui thu mal kha noun mal din a nih ve loh avang emaw, noun leh adjective anga phusa an nih loh vang emawa thu mal atâna ziah zawm ngâi a awm a. Chu lo lehah chuan gender suffix "pui" hi a kawppui thu malah behtir ṭhin a ni bawk a.

Entirna: bialpa, bialnu, monu, mopa, bawngpui, kèlpui...etc.

Source: Mizo Grammar Society
