THU HNUNG BET (SUFFIX) Plural marker “-ho”

✍️ PC. Thang Zikpuia

Mizo ṭawnga “ho” thu mal hrang hrang leh plural marker (suffix) “-ho” chu a sawi fiahna tawi te nen, entirna sentence nen kan târ lang tawh a. Thu hnung bet (suffix) “-ho” hi sawi zui lawk ila a ṭha awm e.

1. Noun-ah a bet

Noun chu pakhat aia tam kan sawina a nih chuan plural marker (suffix) “-ho” hi behtir ṭhin a ni.

Naupang kha ngaihsak rawh.
Naupangho kha ngaihsak rawh.

“Naupang” kan tih ringawt hi chuan noun hi pakhat (singular) kan sawina nge pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina a chiang lo a, “-ho” a beh hi chuan pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina a ni tih a chiang a ni.

2. Noun phrase-ah a bet

Pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina a nih chuan noun phrase-ah pawh “-ho” behtir ṭhin a ni.

Thu awih lo chu hrem rawh.
Thu awih loho chu hrem rawh.

“Thu awih lo” tih ringawt a nih hi chuan noun phrase hi mi pakhat (singular) kan sawina nge pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina tih a chiang lo a, “-ho” kan behtir hi chuan noun phrase hi pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina a ni tih a lang chiang a ni.

3. Noun clause-ah a bet

Pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina a nih chuan noun clause-ah pawh “-ho” behtir ṭhin a ni.

Ka thil lei hi dah fel rawh.
Ka thil leihho hi dah fel rawh.

“Ka thil lei” tih ringawt a nih chuan pakhat (singular) kan sawina nge pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina tih a chiang lo a, “-ho” a beh hi chuan pakhat aia tam kan sawina a ni tih a chiang a ni.

Hetiang hian kan mi sawi emaw, kan thil sawi emaw kha pakhat aia tam (plural) sawina a nih chuan plural marker (suffix) “-ho” hi noun thu malah te, noun phrase-ah te, noun clause-ah te behtir ṭhin a ni.

Note: “-ho” behtir kher loh pawhin kan sentence (context) azirin noun thu mal, noun phrase, noun clause-te hi pakhat aia tam (plural) kan sawina a ni tih a lang thei ṭhin bawk.

Kan chhinchhiah tel atana ṭha chu: hetianga pakhat aia tam sawi nana kan hman, plural marker (suffix) ni lo, Mizo ṭawnga “ho” kan hman ṭhin dangte chu thu mal dang hnungah behtir ve loh tur a ni.
